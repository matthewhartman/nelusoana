About

Born in Romania July 7, 1958 Nelus Oana was a growing talent. He attended the School Of Arts, Unions Cultural Centre, Cluj - Napoca, Romania with professor Vasile Pop in 1980.

In 1981 Nelus graduated from the Faculty of Denstistry, Cluj-Napoca, Romania and for 9 years he participated in solo and group exhibitions in Romania.

In 1989 Nelus lived in Spain and later during that year in November he arrived in Melbourne, Australia.

In 1991 - 1992 he attended Moorabbin College of TAFE, Melbourne, Australia doing Visual Arts.

When Nelus recieved his Bachelor of Arts in Ceramics at Monash University, Melbourne he started various group exhibitions in Melbourne from 1996 through to 2002.

In 1999 he did his first solo show in Australia, Glen Iris Arts Complex, Melbourne. Nelus then progressed doing more solo exhibitions throughout the years.
